1.       DESCRIPTION

When you get the mage stronghold, Lavok gives you an item that teleports
you in (and out) of the planar sphere from wherever you are. You can't exit
to Athkatla however, If you teleported in, you have to teleport out.

You get the item even if you already have the stronghold
and it works in SOA-Areas as well as in TOB-Areas
